About Me



Hello,

I am currently a graduate student in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology (Atlanta), specializing in Computer Graphics.

In the past, I worked as a Technical Director/Artist (Layout/Stereoscopic 3D) at DreamWorks Animation SKG (Redwood City/Bangalore). Watch out for my name in the credits of 'The Penguins of Madagascar'! I graduated from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) - Pilani in 2012 with a Bachelors degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

I have an interest in the areas of Graphics and Vision. I have had the opportunity to work on research and professional projects that attracted me to these interdisciplinary fields.
